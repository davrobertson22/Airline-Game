Everything you need to run an airline

🛫 Fleet Management

Lease or purchase aircraft — narrowbody, widebody, regional jets. Match your fleet to your routes for maximum profit.

🗺️ Route Planning

Open routes between hundreds of real airports. Balance demand, competition, and operating costs to find the most profitable connections.

🏢 Hub Strategy

Build connecting hubs at major airports to unlock network advantages and fill seats through codeshares and passenger flow.

🤝 Alliances

Join or form airline alliances to share routes, boost load factors, and unlock international markets.

⚔️ Competition

Go head-to-head with AI competitors. Undercut fares, steal market share, and defend your best routes.

💰 Finance & Reputation

Manage cash flow, take on debt, and grow your brand reputation. Don't go bankrupt — but don't play it too safe either.

🎫 Loyalty Program

Build a frequent flyer program to drive repeat bookings and keep passengers loyal to your airline over competitors.

⚙️ Operations

Manage gate assignments, maintenance, and turnaround times. Operational efficiency is the difference between profit and loss.

How to play

Start by naming your airline and choosing a home airport. You'll have a small budget to lease your first aircraft and open a few routes. Each week passes automatically — revenue comes in, costs go out, and your airline grows (or struggles).

As you grow, you'll unlock larger aircraft, expand to more airports, build hubs, manage competitor pressure, and invest in your brand. The goal: build the world's best airline without going bankrupt.
